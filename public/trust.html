Trust & boundaries

Clear boundaries make guidance more reliable.

OmanX is designed to support decision quality, preserve context, and signal when official verification is required.

Principles

Scope discipline

OmanX structures support conversations. It does not replace legal, medical, or institutional authorities.

Decision traceability

Conversations, exports, and notes keep rationale visible so teams can review and improve outcomes.

Escalation awareness

Higher-risk topics should move to accountable owners with clear follow-up and official confirmation.
