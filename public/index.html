What a guidance session looks like

User question
"Can I drop below full-time enrollment?"
Risk level
High — this can affect both visa status and scholarship continuity.
Likely rule area
Full-time enrollment requirement tied to your immigration status and sponsor terms.
Next office to contact
Your international student office, before you make any changes to your course load.
Source needed
Your current enrollment terms and immigration record, confirmed directly with that office.

Example shown for a US F-1 scenario — OmanX applies the same structure to UK and Australian rules.
