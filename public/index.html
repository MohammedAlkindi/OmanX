Question
"My scholarship requires full-time enrollment, but one course conflicts with my visa compliance timeline. What should I do first?"

Interpretation
The immediate risk is compliance exposure from registration delay. Scholarship impact is secondary but still time-sensitive.
Recommended next step
Submit a same-day status note to your international office and scholarship sponsor, then request approved alternatives before add/drop closes.
